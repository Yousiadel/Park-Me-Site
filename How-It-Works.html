How it Works!

Learn more about what we do and how we do it by reading the prompts below. Never wait for parking again, go with Spot Finder!

Or, click the button below to start searching for a spot.

Search
Step 1: Find a Parking Space

Search for the perfect parking spot online or by using our real-time App. Find a parking garage based on what you need most: a nearby location, added services, or the best available rates.

Step 2: Book your parking spot

Select date and time and choose a parking space at your preferred location. All you need to do to book is to log into your customer account. Not a member yet – Sign up Now, it’s free!

Step 3: Check in and park

At the time of your reservation, head to your chosen location. You can easily check- in at the parking garage from your smartphone.
